Good to Know

What We Won't Remove

For safety and environmental reasons, we're unable to haul hazardous materials. These items require special disposal facilities.

✕Paint, propane, paint thinner
✕Pesticides, fertilizers, rodent killer
✕Drain cleaners & ammonia
✕Batteries & aerosol cans
✕Floor cleaner
✕Fluorescent bulbs
✕Thermometers & thermostats
✕Iodine
